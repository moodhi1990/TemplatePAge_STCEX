The 9th Saudi Technical Conference & Exhibition STCEX 2020

Training for the future: The knowledge economy and sustainable development

10 - 12 March 2020

Time 8AM – 10PM

King Faisal Conference Hall, Riyadh, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

The 9th Saudi Technical Conference and Exhibition (STCEX 2020) is an international conference that aims to attract selective experts, researchers, local and international business representatives and government officials to share information, experiences and the latest developments in Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET).

STCEX 2020 addresses a number of scientific papers focusing on three main topics:

  • Prospecting future skills and occupations.
  • The role of TVET in achieving sustainable development.
  • Investing in TVET.

Training workshops will be delivered throughout the conference and there will be exhibition opportunities for business professionals and relevant government agencies.

Objectives:

  1. Sharing TVET expertise, latest developments, experiences and initiatives.
  2. Discussing the relevant TVET issues, including staffing requirements, programs, curricula and approaches in TVET associated with the skills of future occupations.
  3. Discussing the main challenges facing TVET in the context of Industry 4.0.
  4. Presenting distinctive experiences of investment and privatization related to TVET.
  5. Discussing the issues of cooperation, integration and entrepreneurship programs among training sectors and employment agencies.
  6. Reviewing models of the latest local and international training programs

Main topics of the conference:

  1. Determining future skills and occupations:
      2. This topic explores skills and occupations that will be needed in the future and examines the mechanisms of identifying and suitably adapting the required training programs. It will also explore the role of TVET in engendering the required competencies. The challenges that face TVET in the Industry 4.0 era will be addressed under this topic.
  2. The role of TVET in achieving sustainable development:
      3. This topic details the contributions of TVET in developing training modules to enable young people to cope with the requirements of the labor market. The topic also addresses TVET's contribution in encouraging creativity and innovation in promising fields and in developing the training curricula and appropriate training approaches. It also discusses the role of professional certificates in the training-based standard.
  3. Investing in TVET:
      4. The last topic identifies the most prominent strategies and experiences of investment related to TVET. It also focuses on entrepreneurship and measuring the Return on Investment (ROI). The topic also discusses the models on partnership and privatization in TVET.
