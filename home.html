STCEX

The Ninth Saudi Technical Conference and Exhibition (STCEX 2019) provides a platform for bringing together a select group of distinguished international and local experts to exchange ideas, experiences, and initiatives about the 21st century skills and their associated new developments and future trends in the field of Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET); with the aim of advancing ongoing TVET’s development efforts in KSA and enhancing its contribution to the achievement of KSA-Vision (2030) aspirations.
